Description

The HPC Fundamentals course is an introduction to High Performance Computing (HPC) and its various components. It covers the fundamental concepts of HPC, the architecture of modern CPUs, memory, and networks, as well as the architecture of HPC systems and the applications they are used for. The second part of the course focuses on HPC programming, including multi-core programming with OpenMP, programming for distributed systems with MPI, and programming accelerators with OpenACC. These topics will be covered through lectures and lab/demo sessions. The course is designed to provide students with hands-on experience in developing high-performance, parallel applications..
